About us

Our mission is to provide unforgettable moments in a luxurious atmosphere. We offer exceptional service that combines modernity with traditional values of hospitality.


Our history

Martyard Hotel was established in 2010 as a place that combines elegance with modern design. Founded by the young and ambitious Marta Sajnóg, who began her career with a position in the sales department of one of Warsaw's hotels, the hotel was intended from the start to reflect her vision of exceptional hospitality and luxury available to everyone. With her commitment, passion for the hospitality industry and exceptional management skills, Marta quickly rose through the ranks, gaining experience that she decided to turn into the creation of her own venture.


Our team

Our team are professionals with a passion for creating memorable experiences. The hotel's director, Marta Sajnóg, with over 20 years of experience in the industry, leads us towards success. Our staff are trained to provide the highest level of service, ensuring that every guest feels special.
